About

Scary Romantic is a feature film production company based in Los Angeles. We find and develop top-tier scripts built to resonate — stories rooted in authenticity, real-life themes, and the emotional undercurrents that modern audiences actually connect with.

We secure financing, get projects greenlit, and bring in the right producers and crew to execute at the highest level. Fully equipped with the latest technology and a creative edge that sets our work apart.

We understand the market and what people want. Every project we take on is driven by a commitment to craft, cultural relevance, and stories that hit close to home.
